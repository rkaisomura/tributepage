Tribute page: Marie Fredriksson

Swedish pop singer, songwriter, pianist and painter, who was best known internationally as the lead vocalist of pop rock duo Roxette, which she formed in 1986 with Per Gessle.
Marie Fredriksson

Timeline of Marie:

  • 1958: Marie was born in Össjö, Sweden.
  • 1978–1984: Early work
  • 1984–1989: Solo work and Roxette
  • 1989–1992: Roxette's international breakthrough
  • 1992–2002: Continued solo workh
  • 2002–2003: Brain tumour diagnosis and aftermath
  • 2004–2008: Return to creativity
  • 2008–2016: Return to touring, Roxette reunion and Nu!
  • 2019: Marie passed away in Djursholm, Sweden with 61 years old.
